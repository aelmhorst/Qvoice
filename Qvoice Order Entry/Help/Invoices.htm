Generating invoices

Generating invoices is simply going through the process of printing them out so that they can be mailed or emailed to the customer.

To generate Invoices:

  1. Click TOOLS -> Invoice Generation.
  2. The system will load the invoice generation screen.
  3. Click "Next" to generate the invoices.
  4. The system will tell you how many invoices it generated.
  5. The next screen will allow you to change the printing preferences. The default preferences will normally be fine and you will not need to change them.
  6. Make sure the invoice forms are properly inserted into the printer, with a blank sheet of paper on top for the invoice summary report. It may be more convenient to have the invoices print out on a separate printer or from a separate paper tray from the summary report. This can easily be set up. Contact your Qvoice administrator for details.
  7. Click the "Next Button.
  8. The invoices and summary report will then print.
  9. Do not click the "Finish Batch" button until you have verified that all invoices have properly printed. If they have not, you may press the "BACK" button to go back and then the "NEXT" button to reprint the entire batch. If only a small percentage of invoices did not print correctly, it may be more convenient to reprint them individually. See the following section Reprinting Invoices for details
  10. Once everything has correctly printed, click the "Finish Batch" button.
  11. The system will then finish the process and return you to the main Qvoice screen.

Notes:

Once you have started invoice generation and pressed "NEXT' on the first screen, you should not exit the process until you have printed everything out. If you attempt to exit the invoice generation screen prematurely, the system will warn you multiple times and then give you a Batch ID to give to your Qvoice administrator. If this happens, your administrator will have to fix the unfinished batch of invoices, so keep track of the Batch ID.

Reprinting Invoices

Looking up a customer's past invoices

To lookup a customer's past invoices or to reprint an invoice:

  1. Click TOOL -> Reports -> Invoice Lookup (Or F3).
  2. Select the customer and the time frame on the resulting screen.
  3. The system will load a standard report viewer, with all of the invoices for that customer and time frame. For more information on the report viewer, click here.
  4. You may use the navigation tree on the left or the page navigation buttons to navigate to the invoice you would like to see. You may also search by any of the fields on the order, such as purchase order, job name, etc. to get to the order or invoice you would like to see.
  5. If you would like to reprint the invoice, you should note the start and end page number of the invoice and then click the printer icon at the top of the screen.
  6. If you need the invoice printed out on a real invoice form, insert them now into the invoice printer tray.
  7. Enter the start and end page number in the print range fields and then click "OK".
  8. The invoices will print to the invoice printer.
  9. To exit the Invoice Lookup Report, click the standard Windows close window button at the upper right hand corner of the window.
