Using the Report Viewer

The Crystal Report viewer window is used for many of the reports in Qvoice. Use the illustration below to see some of the capabilities of the window. Letters A through E match to a description in the text below.

A. Some of the preview windows will contain a tree-view to the left. For large reports, this allows easy navigation to a particular subsection, such as a specific invoice.
B. The current page and page navigation is shown here. Click the right or left inner arrows to advance or go back a page at a time. Click the right or left outer arrows to go to the first or last page.
C. The printer icon allows the report to be printed out. You will be allowed to only print out a subset of the pages, if you so choose. The button to the right of the print icon will allow you to switch printers.
D. You can change the zoom of the report here. You may magnify or reduce the size of the report.
E. You may enter a search word here and press enter or click the Binocular icon. The report viewer will search for the word. This allows for easy searching for a particular order by jobname, countertop color, or any other word that appears in the report. You may continue searching throughout the report by repeatedly pressing enter or clicking the icon.
